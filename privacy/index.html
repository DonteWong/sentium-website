Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

Sentium is designed with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have as a user.

Data We Collect

Journal Entries

Your journal entries are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your journal entries on any server we operate.

AI Processing

When you submit a journal entry, the text of that entry is sent to Anthropic's API for the purpose of generating thematic tags. This is the only time your entry text leaves your device. Anthropic's data handling practices are governed by their privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

No Account Data

Sentium does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

No Analytics

Sentium does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage behavior, session length, or in-app actions.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Name or email address
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Behavioral or usage analytics
  • Health or biometric data
  • Financial information

Third-Party Services

Sentium uses the following third-party service:

Anthropic API — Used exclusively for AI-powered tag generation from journal entry text. Entry text is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing and is not retained by Sentium. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

No other third-party services are used in Sentium.

Data Storage and Security

All journal data is stored locally on your device. Sentium does not operate any servers that store your personal data. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

Sentium is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
